House of Tarab Announces Debut
Fashion Show & Cocktail Reception

Atlanta, Georgia — July 16, 2026

ATLANTA, GA —

House of Tarab, Atlanta's newest luxury made-to-order fashion house, will present its debut collection at an exclusive fashion show and cocktail reception on Thursday, July 16, 2026. Founded by Joy, House of Tarab introduces a new standard of elevated workwear, formal wear, and evening gowns — each piece commissioned to order, crafted to the client's exact measurements, and designed for moments that must not be forgotten.

About the Show

The debut presentation will take place in Atlanta, Georgia. Doors open at 7:00 PM, with the runway show beginning at 7:30 PM. A cocktail reception follows at 8:00 PM, giving guests the opportunity to view the collection up close, meet the founder, and learn about the commission process. General admission tickets are $10 minimum with an option to donate more to support the future of House of Tarab.

About the Collection

The debut collection spans three categories: The Authority (elevated workwear), The Ceremony (formal wear), and The Evening (floor-length gowns). Each design is produced a maximum of ten times — ten garments, each made to the exact measurements of the woman who commissions it. The collection reflects Joy's belief that a woman's presence is the most powerful thing she wears, and that clothing made specifically for her body is not a luxury — it is a standard.

About House of Tarab

House of Tarab takes its name from the Arabic word Tarab — a joy so transcendent it moves everyone in the room. Founded by Joy, whose name is the direct English translation of Tarab, the brand was built around the fullest, most elevated version of what joy can mean. Every piece is made to order in Atlanta, Georgia. Commission timelines range from six weeks for workwear to three months for bespoke designs.
